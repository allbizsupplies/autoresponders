Common Problems

We print your files as supplied. Sometimes important bits are missing, or not quite right. Read our product help guides and general print FAQ so you can supply print ready PDF files. Each online product has a help guide:

shop.allbizsupplies.biz

Common job-ruining problems:

  • Fuzzy/jaggy images (low resolution)
  • Wrong size art for the product
  • No bleed
  • No trim marks 

Get a Hardcopy Proof

Your document might look good on screen, but screen does not equal print (they work differently). To avoid disappointment, get a hardcopy proof.
